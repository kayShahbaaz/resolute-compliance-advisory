# Brightpath — ISO 27001 and SOC 2 Assessment

**Project Code:** RCA-P1  
**Client Industry:** IT Services, Bengaluru, India  
**Engagement Type:** Gap Assessment + SOC 2 Readiness Review  
**Duration:** October 2023 – February 2024  
**Role:** GRC Analyst  
**Frameworks:** ISO 27001:2022, SOC 2

---

## Engagement Overview

This engagement involved a comprehensive gap assessment against ISO 27001:2022 and a SOC 2 readiness review for a Bengaluru-based IT services firm. The assessment identified significant compliance gaps and critical security risks requiring remediation.

### Key Findings

- **ISO 27001 Conformance:** 37.6% (35/93 controls implemented)
- **SOC 2 Security Readiness:** Partially Ready
- **Critical Risk Identified:** Former employee credential exposure identified as a live Critical risk
- **Risk Profile:** 27 risks across 9 domains

## Assessment Scope

- **ISO 27001:2022** – Full control assessment across all 4 domains (A.5 – A.14)
- **SOC 2** – Security (CC) criteria alignment
- **Physical & Environmental Security** – Data center and office assessments
- **Access Control & Identity Management** – IAM maturity review
- **Incident Management & Response** – Incident response capability evaluation

---

## Next Steps & Recommendations

The remediation roadmap prioritises addressing the following:

1. **Immediate (Critical)** – Revoke former employee credentials; implement credential lifecycle management
2. **Phase 1 (6 months)** – Implement access control mechanisms; establish SOC 2 foundation
3. **Phase 2 (12 months)** – Complete ISO 27001 control implementation; obtain SOC 2 Type I certification
4. **Phase 3 (18 months)** – Achieve ISO 27001:2022 certification; transition to SOC 2 Type II
